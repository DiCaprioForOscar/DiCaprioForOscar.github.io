Nissan Skyline R32

I try to give you a bit of an info about a car which has a special place in my heart

History of this vehicle

In August of 1989, Nissan revived the Name "Skyline" again with the R32. R32 stands short for E-BNR32 which is the name of the chassis. Since the history could only be told in hours i tried to make a short version. The car featured a all wheel drive system, a big advantage in the Racing series on the other Hand the car was suffering because of his weight which could be changed of course. They are many people who praise the Skyline series as the best cars of all time which is a bit overexaggerated in my opnion.

  • A few short facts

    The Skyline series itself played also a role in the early 2000s Films and also Japanese Manga Series.

  • Designer of this vehicle

    The person who designed this car was one of the head Engineers at Nissan at that time, his name is Kozo Watanabe. He was also responsible for the design for the R33 and the R34.
